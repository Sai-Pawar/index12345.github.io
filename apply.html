Eligibility Criteria

Each financial aid program may have specific eligibility criteria. Common factors include:

  • Academic performance and achievements.
  • Financial need based on FAFSA or other application forms.
  • Enrollment status (full-time or part-time).

Application Process

Follow these steps to apply for financial aid:

  1. Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) or relevant financial aid application forms.
  2. Submit any required documents, such as tax returns or proof of income.
  3. Explore and apply for specific scholarship or grant programs separately, if applicable.
  4. Monitor communication from the financial aid office for updates on your application status.
